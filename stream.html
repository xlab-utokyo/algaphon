HOW IT WORKS

Oyxgen bubbles that leave algae filaments ring at ring at minnaert frequency. We render these bubbles audible thrugh a hydrophone, for the audience to listen to photosynthesis/carbon conversion in real-time.

Your audio recording is converted and played as photosynthetic radiation (PAR) in our saltwater aquariums. Our macroalgae aquariums (with Sea Lettuce and Red Ogo species) are in Tokyo (running 9am-1pm Linz Time) and New York (running from 1pm-5pm Linz time).
