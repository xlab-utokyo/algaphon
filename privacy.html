PRIVACY POLICY

Algaphon Privacy Policy

The Algaphon ("Project") has established the following privacy policy ("Privacy Policy") regarding the handling of user information (including personal information as defined in Article 2 of the Act on the Protection of Personal Information (Act No. 57 of May 30, 2003)) on this website and in the experience of the interactive work Algaphon ("Art Work") exhibited on this website.

Article 1: Acquisition of user information
In this Privacy Policy, "user information" means information that identifies a user and that is obtained by the Project in accordance with this Privacy Policy. User information obtained by this project through browsing this website and experiencing the work is as follows

Audio recording from the visitor
Email address, Name and City of the visitors
User's cookie and other activity history

Article 2: Purpose of Use
User information on this website will be used for the following purposes

Experience the Art Work
Understand how users use the website and experience the Art Work, and help improve the website and the Art Work.
Use for research purposes.
For other purposes incidental or related to the above items

Article 3: Audio Acquisition
The content of the recording is stored on U-Tokyo server and will not be shared with anyone without de-identification. Publically available photosynthesis bubble audio responses as a result of user recording (shown on Algaphon archive) are also de-identified publically.

Article 4: Inquiries
If you have any questions about the handling of user information, please contact the following
algaphon[at]xlab.iii.u-tokyo.ac.jp

Article 5: Changes to this privacy policy
The Project may make changes to this Privacy Policy, in whole or in part, as necessary. In the event of such changes, the contents of such changes will be posted on this website.
