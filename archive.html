ALGAPHON LIVE STREAM ARCHIVE ABOUT

PAST RECORDINGS

This list will be refreshed by the end of each day during the Ars Electronica Festival. All names are anonymized to the public.

We convert audio recordings to Photosynthetically Active Radiation (PAR) and play it as light to the algae in one of our remote aquariums (based in Tokyo and New York). Since photosynthesis and consecutive oxygen saturation takes time, we translated 3sec visitor voice messages to 45 minutes of light variations. We only make available algae bubbles responses identifiable by ID to the individual person who recorded the message. No visitor voice recordings are posted here.
